Barber PAT

This is a simple appointment booking application. The program is 
intended to allow users to book and specify which haircut they 
want to do at their next appointment. Users will pick the hairstyle,
the barber and the date of the appointment. This application 
will reduce the possibility of a barber or user forgetting about 
the haircut they have scheduled. It provides a user-friendly way
of for users to view and delete any of their appointments.
